SHORT STORY COVERS

As you can probably tell I was inspired by Saul Bass, I like how most of not of his designs tell a story but leave enough room for you to interpret what it could mean; and that was something I wanted to emulate that in this cover. Just a simple story of a man who just wanted to get on with his day.
